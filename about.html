About Colorado Music Network

When I first moved to Colorado (from Binghamton, NY) I was looking for some kind of website that had a list of venues. I never found it. At least there was nothing that was comprehensive. Then within a year, I found myself on the other end, booking bands to play at the Denver Art Society. Again, I found a variety of different sources, and none were what I was looking for. This site aims to be what I was looking for. A central place where anyone can see an updated list of bands and venues in Colorado. - Ebenezer
