Poor Bodhi

Sounds for your dome:
Poor Bodhi blends several genres to make a unique sound. They formed in 2012 when singer Jenee Donnelson met guitarist Ebenezer Ingalsbe at the Denver Art Society. Soon Dustin Adams started sitting in on the drums (after another drummer didnt' work out). And, after a couple different bassists in the first few years, David Baily joined to throw down the low end.
